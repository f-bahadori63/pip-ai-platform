# Principles

## AP-01 Simplicity

Prefer simple and maintainable solutions.

---

## AP-02 Modular Design

Each module shall have a single responsibility.

---

## AP-03 Scalability

The architecture must support future expansion without major redesign.

---

## AP-04 API First

All modules communicate through well-defined APIs.

---

## AP-05 AI Ready

The architecture must support AI services without changing core modules.

---

## AP-06 Security by Design

Authentication, authorization and audit logging are built into the architecture.

---

## AP-07 Data Integrity

A single source of truth must exist for every business entity.

---

## AP-08 Observability

Logging, monitoring and error tracking must be available across all services.

---

## AP-09 Technology Independence

Business logic should remain independent of UI and database technologies.

---

## AP-10 Extensibility

Future modules can be added with minimal impact on existing components.
