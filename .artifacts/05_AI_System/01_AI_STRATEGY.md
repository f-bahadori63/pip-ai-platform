# Purpose

Define the AI strategy for integrating artificial intelligence capabilities into PIP.

---

# AI Vision

PIP uses artificial intelligence to transform project data into intelligent insights, predictions, and decision support.

---

# AI Objectives

## 1. Decision Support

Provide project managers with intelligent recommendations.

---

## 2. Risk Prediction

Identify potential project risks before they become critical issues.

---

## 3. Knowledge Utilization

Reuse historical project knowledge and lessons learned.

---

## 4. Process Automation

Reduce repetitive project management activities.

---

# AI Capabilities

## Data Analysis

Analyze:

- Project performance

- Schedule trends

- Cost information

- Risk indicators

---

## Intelligent Assistance

Provide:

- Recommendations

- Explanations

- Reports

---

## Predictive Intelligence

Support:

- Delay prediction

- Risk forecasting

- Performance analysis

---

# AI Principles

- Human decision remains final

- AI recommendations must be explainable

- Data quality affects AI quality

- Security and privacy are mandatory

---

# AI Development Approach

PIP AI capabilities will be developed incrementally:

Phase 1:

- AI assistant

- Knowledge search

Phase 2:

- Recommendations

- Analytics

Phase 3:

- Predictive intelligence
