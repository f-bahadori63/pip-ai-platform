# Problem Overview

Industrial EPC projects face complex challenges in planning, execution, monitoring, and decision making.

Existing project management tools mainly store information but do not provide intelligent analysis and proactive recommendations.

---

# Main Problems

## 1. Fragmented Information

Project data exists in different systems, files, emails, and spreadsheets.

Impact:

- Lack of transparency

- Slow reporting

- Data inconsistency

---

## 2. Reactive Project Management

Many decisions are made after problems occur.

Impact:

- Schedule delays

- Cost increase

- Risk escalation

---

## 3. Manual Reporting

Project teams spend significant time preparing reports manually.

Impact:

- Reduced productivity

- Delayed management decisions

---

## 4. Lack of Project Intelligence

Historical project knowledge is not effectively reused.

Impact:

- Repeated mistakes

- Loss of organizational experience

---

## 5. Limited Integration

Engineering, procurement, construction, quality, and cost data are not connected.

Impact:

- Incomplete project visibility

- Poor coordination

---

# Problem Statement Summary

Organizations need an intelligent platform that integrates project information and transforms project data into actionable insights.
