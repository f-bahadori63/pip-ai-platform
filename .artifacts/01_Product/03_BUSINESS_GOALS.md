# Business Goals

## BG-01: Improve Project Control

Provide real-time visibility of project progress, cost, schedule, risks, and performance.

---

## BG-02: Reduce Project Delays

Use data analysis and AI assistance to identify potential delays and provide early warnings.

---

## BG-03: Improve Decision Making

Provide managers with accurate information and intelligent recommendations.

---

## BG-04: Standardize Project Processes

Create common workflows for planning, execution, monitoring, and reporting.

---

## BG-05: Preserve Organizational Knowledge

Store project experiences, lessons learned, and historical data for future projects.

---

## BG-06: Increase Project Efficiency

Reduce manual reporting and repetitive project management activities through automation.
