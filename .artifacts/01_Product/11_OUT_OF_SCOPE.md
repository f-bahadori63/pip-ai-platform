# Out Of Scope

## Document Information

| Item | Value |

|---|---|

| Project | PIP - Project Intelligence Platform |

| Version | 1.0 |

---

# Purpose

This document defines features and capabilities that are not included in PIP Version 1.0.

---

# Excluded Features

## 1. Full ERP System

Not included:

- Accounting

- Payroll

- Financial transactions

- Enterprise resource planning

---

## 2. Human Resource Management

Not included:

- Employee attendance

- Salary management

- Recruitment processes

---

## 3. Supply Chain Execution

Not included:

- Supplier payment processing

- Inventory management

- Warehouse operations

---

## 4. Autonomous Project Execution

Not included:

- Automatic approval of decisions

- Fully autonomous project management

- Direct control of external systems

---

## 5. Advanced External Data Analytics

Not included:

- Market analysis

- Competitor intelligence

- External business forecasting

---

## Scope Control Rule

Any excluded capability can be considered for future versions through formal change management and product roadmap review.
